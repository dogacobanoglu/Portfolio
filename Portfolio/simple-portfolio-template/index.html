About Me

I'm a Columbia Engineering undergraduate student studying Computer Science and Entrepreneurship in New York City. Originally from Turkey, I have grown up in the Netherlands, Brunei, and Oman, providing me a multifaceted diverse skill pallatte that I'm excited to utilize. I have had a wide rangoe of experience in full-stack development with apps and software tools, machine learning applications and model tuning, and end-to-end testing. My drive lies in innovating and curating conscience engineering based products while empowering women in STEM and taking real-world problems into consideration. I spend my free time creating art of many sorts, I love painting and ceramics - Refik Anadol is one of my favorite artists. I also enjoy travelling as much as I can, and sailing!
